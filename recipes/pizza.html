Pizza Recipe

Description

This is the best pizza recipe that I've ever used. The most important parts of the recipe that make it so good are the kind of flour used and the temperature. It's also useful to measure your ingredients by weight instead of volume. This is important because powdered ingredients like flour or salt can condense under their own weight and this can affect measurements based on volume.

The flour used in this recipe is a 00 flour. This is a very fine kind of white flour. Using this kind of flour instead of an all-purpose flour is what makes the texture of the dough perfect. This kind of flour used to be hard to find, but these days you can find it in pretty much any grocery store you'll go to.

Very high tempretures are extremely important for making the perfect pizza. The easiest way of achieving the right temperature without a pizza oven is by using your grill. Grills can get up to a much higher tempreature than most ovens that people have in their homes.

Ingredients

  • 153 g 00 flour
  • 153 g all-purpose flour
  • 8 g salt
  • 2 g active dry yeast
  • 4 g extra-virgin olive oil
  • 200 g warm water

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Combine warm water, yeast and oil. Let sit until yeast blooms.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and knead for 3 minutes.
  4. Let rest for 15 minutes, knead for another 3 minutes and then let it rest for another 30 minutes.
  5. Knead for another 3 minutes than let it rest for 2 hours.
  6. Prepare pizza and bake on the grill at 550 F.
