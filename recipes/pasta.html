Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the feta and tomato pasta that got popular recently after it went viral on TikTok. While many people immediately criticized it for not being authentic italian cuisine, but I think that these people are misunderstanding why the recipe went viral. The biggest draw of this recipe is how quick and easy it is.

The active part of this recipe doesn't take more than five minutes and even some of that can be omittid if you don't use fresh garlic. The recipe is also very costumizable if someone does want to make it more impressive.

One important tip that I have for leftovers is to store the pasta and the sauce separetely instead of combinig them. If you warm up the pasta and the sauce separetely the next day instead of just microvawing them together, it will be less likely to dry out and more likely to taste just as good as it did on the first day.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta
  • 1 brick of feta cheese
  • 2 boxes of tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • pinch of pepper
  • red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 F.
  2. Peel the garlic and wash the tomatoes.
  3. Combine all the ingredienta in a pan.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
  5. While the sauce is in the oven, cook the pasta.
  6. Serve sauce on top of pasta without combinig them.
